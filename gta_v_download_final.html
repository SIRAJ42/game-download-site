Game Overview

Type of game: Action, Adventure

PC Release Date: September 17, 2013

Developer/Publishers: Rockstar North, Rockstar Games

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It's the seventh main entry in the Grand Theft Auto series and is known for its sprawling open world, engaging storyline, and online multiplayer component, Grand Theft Auto Online. The game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, based on Southern California, and features three playable protagonists: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips, each with their own unique backgrounds and skills.
